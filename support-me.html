About

Our mission with this tool is to make it more convenient for the user to visualize what they want their Voron color theme to look like. Users can now create and visualize everything from the default two color groups (primary and accent color) to a very unique looking build, where up to 9 different groups of parts can be colored (this holds for Voron V0.1).
