WELCOME TO 3D Printer Color Configurator

Welcome to 3D Printer Color Configurator Tool (3DP-CCT). Using this tool, you can design your very own color theme of any 3D printer that is currently available on this site (and in the future mods as well), to your liking. Currently we support color configuring all voron printers. Check our roadmap for coming printers, mods, features and fixes.

About

Our mission with this tool is to make it more convenient for the user to visualize what they want their Voron color theme to look like. Users can now create and visualize everything from the default four color groups (primary and accent color + chain and frame) to a very unique looking build, where up to 8 different groups of parts can be colored.
