About Me

I'm a student at University of Copenhagen, where I study Computer Science at Bachelor level. My interests however lie within Web Development and the DIY world, which includes technologies as 3D printing, CNC as well as laser cutting and engraving.

I love being able to fulfill my creativity through these technologies, which includes all from building and upgrading the machines, solving problems as well as tuning, to drawing functional and non-functional models in Fusion 360 or TinkerCad and watch them come to life. What comes after is post-prosessing and here I like to make my creations as perfect as they can be. For example with 3D prints, this includes sanding, priming, hand painting as well as varnishing.

When designing websites, drawing models for printing, etc. my focus and goal is always to make the end-product as user friendly and convenient for the end-user as possible.

My mission with this tool is therefore to make it more convenient for the user to visualize how they want their Voron color theme to look like.
